Overview of Executive Programs

Course Requirements

You are required to complete the Evidence of Capacity Report (ECR) and submit all relevant documentary evidence to support your application.

Documentary Evidence may include:
  • Qualifications & Certificates
  • Licenses & Tickets
  • Curriculum Vitae
  • References & Supporting Letter
  • Employment Records & Reports
  • Supervisor Reports
  • Professional Development Certificates

To be eligible to apply for this qualification you are required to have the following:
  • 3 Years work experience as a manager/supervisor of a business relevant to your field of study
  • Completed a formal qualification in management
  • Have completed a Workplace health and Safety Certificate

In addition, you will be required to submit a formal report outlining your experience and how it related to the topics of the qualification.
