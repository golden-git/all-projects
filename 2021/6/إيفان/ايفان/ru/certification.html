EMD801 Executive Diploma of Hotel Management (Доказательная база данных)

Управление отелем сосредоточено на надзоре за работой отеля, мотеля или объекта размещения. Сюда могут входить такие области, как маркетинг и рекламная деятельность, обучение и набор персонала, решение вопросов, связанных с клиентами, и надзор за повседневными операциями.

MD802 Executive Diploma of Restaurant Management (Доказательные данные)

CEffective restaurant management involves being able to understand and interpret challenges, such as public relations, inventory, staff, and customer service. In some instances, a restaurant owner may also serve as the manager. A strong manager is an essential component of a successful restaurant.

EMD803 Executive Diploma of Resort Management (Evidence Based)

Resort management is part of the hospitality and lodging industry. ... Resort Management includes instruction in hospitality administration, hotel management, restaurant and food services management, facilities planning, leisure studies, recreation administration, marketing.

EMD804 Executive Diploma of Kitchen Management (Evidence Based)

Understanding how a Kitchen functions is a major part of the role of a Kitchen Manager. Being a Chef is only part of the process. There is a level of skill to running a kitchen that comes from years of training and on the job experience. Leadership plays a vital part in any Kitchen operations.

EMD805 Executive Diploma of Event Management (Evidence Based)

The Event Management program is designed to identify the expertise and skills needed to run and manage events of 50 people or more. The knowledge and skills needed to effectively manage and perform duties to ensure a successful event is an ever-changing skill set, depending on location and theme.

GDD901 Graduate Diploma of Hospitality Management (Evidence Based)

Hospitality Management is the study of the hospitality industry. ... Hospitality management covers hotels, restaurants, cruise ships, amusement parks, destination marketing organizations, convention centers, country clubs and many more facilities and locations.

Course Requirements

You are required to complete the Evidence of Capacity Report (ECR) and submit all relevant documentary evidence to support your application.

Documentary Evidence may include:
  • Qualifications & Certificates
  • Licenses & Tickets
  • Curriculum Vitae
  • References & Supporting Letter
  • Employment Records & Reports
  • Supervisor Reports
  • Professional Development Certificates

To be eligible to apply for this qualification you are required to have the following:
  • 3 Years work experience as a manager/supervisor of a business relevant to your field of study
  • Completed a formal qualification in management
  • Have completed a Workplace health and Safety Certificate

In addition, you will be required to submit a formal report outlining your experience and how it related to the topics of the qualification.
