What is a student application?

Taleb is an integrated educational platform that provides a range of diverse and pioneering services, bringing together a large number of students, teachers and institutions, and provides the opportunity to learn from one place.

The application aims to facilitate the scientific life with a comprehensive and easy provision that enables users to obtain the required educational service. It also allows those with educational, qualification and training needs, through a specialized team that provides interesting, likable and facilitating educational methods for them.

The application is concerned with the quality of education, and aims to improve and develop the educational process from a distance, as it attracts teachers, to encourage them to spread their knowledge and benefit thousands and millions of students, and to allow parents to manage their children's educational affairs.

For the sake of development and development, the application allows institutes and institutions to present their various educational courses and programs, so that the student can sip from them what he wants and reach his goal with love and determination. There is no greater challenge than self-improvement and development through education.

Studying, understanding, memorizing, training and succeeding are now easier and closer than ever before with the Taleb app.
